    About

    Who am I?

    I'm Jackson, a full time student majoring in Software Technologies and minoring in Advanced Software Technologies as well as Graphic Design.


    For the past few years I've developed quite the interest in software environments and I hope to one day give people the opportunity to express themselves via an interactive medium.


    As for motion graphics and graphic design, I see them as mediums that allow people to express themselves in any way they wish.
    Graphic design essentially serves as a versatile sandbox which is why it appeals to me.

    What do I do?

    I had previously been employed as a Test Engineer and a Software Engineer. I also have had the wonderful opportunity to solve various unique problems alongside a team, generating elegant solutions and creating beautiful code (with the help of others of course.)


    I've also created for some wonderful people such as ilmango, docm77, Rainbxy, Mavro, etc. Collaborating on a project is really enjoyable, and I do hope to have the opportunity to continue collaborating with others as it gives them opportunity to convey what they want to convey.

    Emotion through design?

    Everyone has an emotive response associated with their brand, for some it may be lighthearted, humourous and energetic. For others it may be chill, relaxed, and serious.


    Delivering and expressing yourself is something I believe is important, and doing so through a visual medium is an amazing way of doing so.

    Where to from here?

    That's up for you to decide! If you have any questions, concerns, enquiries, or even a funny meme don't hesitate to contact me.
