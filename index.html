    About

    Who am I?

    Hello! I'm Jackson, a full time student majoring in Software Technologies and minoring in Advanced Software Technologies and Graphic Design.


    For a few years now I've developed quite the interest in software environments and I wish to one day give people the opportunity to express themselves via an interactive medium. As for motion graphics and graphic design, I see them as mediums that allow people to express themselves in any way they wish. Graphics essentially serves as a versatile sandbox which is why it appeals to me.


    What do I do?

    I had previously been employed as a Test Engineer and a Software Engineer. I also have had the wonderful opportunity to solve various unique problems alongside a team, generating elegant solutions and creating beautiful code (with the help of others of course.)


    Commission work for some wonderful people such as ilmango, docm77, Rainbxy, Mavro, etc., and collaborating on a project is really enjoyable, I do hope to have the opportunity to continue collaborating with others as it gives me the opportunity to let them convey what they want to.


    Where to from here?

    Currently I'm focussing on university and engaging with communities. During the holidays I do aim to develop a lot of projects I have in my backlog.



    This portfolio is for DVB102. In future this site may change to further reflect myself as a Software Engineer with a developed aesthetic and Brand.
