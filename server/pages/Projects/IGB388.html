Dead Bread Requisition - IGB388

Myself and 4 others were tasked with further developing a VR prototype, of which my prototype was chosen.

The VR game places the player in an apocalyptic environment where they are tasked with collecting a list of supplies from a supermarket. While doing so they must defend themselves from zombies.

Several areas and models were created to give a clear indication to the locations of different supplies and to add to the shopping environment.
A full layout was established over time and vaguely followed initial designs.

There were many many bugs during development, of which one of the most niche yet annoying bugs involved the player colliders, where the head and body had their own colliders.
This resulted in some weird interactions with the walls as the center collider was aligned to the center of the play area. This was changed to only be dependant on the VR headset's position.

Overall this project gave great insight into VR development, and is one I'm much more proud of working on.
