Project Proposal

Mobile gaming takes an enormous percentage of the gaming demographic, 81% of north americans on the platform engage in mobile gaming, which is also the fastest growing gaming platform in EU (Google, 2021). A lot of people play mobile games, and a lot of mobile games come riddled with ads.

I hope to mitigate this problem by introducing a simple collection of games without any monetization. Minesweeper and Sodoku are two prime examples of games that I personally feel shouldn\'t have any advertising involved.
According to MistPlay (2023), video ads within the mobile market is the most popular form of ad revenue shortly followed by perodic forced ads between rounds, levels, etc. By removing ads entirely and documenting my progress on various platforms, I believe it\'s possible to gain publicity within the mobile game development ecosystem.

A minimalistic minesweeper and sodoku game will demonstrate core skills to a job provider while delivering a positive user experience for those who choose to download.
